Title of project: Counting steps using custom filtering

Instruction: 
Use a smartphone accelerometer with Sense-it to capture 5 walking signals and record a reference (manually calculated steps). Detect the steps using custom filtering and count them (automatically). Compare the accuracy of your step count with the reference.

Authors: 
Bc. Veronika Pošustová, Bc. Jana Schwarzerová

The code has only Czech comments, because instruction was in czech language. 
Results from this toolbox is in the presentation "Technicka_zprava.pdf"
